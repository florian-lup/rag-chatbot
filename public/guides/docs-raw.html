    Get started

    Welcome

    Anara is the place where people and AI do research work together. It’s an AI-enabled workspace that helps you find, understand, organize and produce scientific content. Whether you’re part of a research team in industry, pursuing a PhD at a university lab, or beginning your master’s degree, Anara is designed to support your work from start to finish.

    ​
    Key features

    We offer several features to help you get your research work done faster:
    • Get concise, scholarly answers to any question. When you ask a question in the chat Anara searches major academic databases like PubMed, arXiv and JSTOR to deliver a comprehensive response.
    • Understand any file or group of files, instantly. Whether it’s a dense 200-page textbook, a new clinical study, or a technical research paper, just import it to Anara and start asking questions.
    • Trusted sources and citations in every response. Every response includes citations so you can verify accuracy and ensure your work maintains the highest standard of academic integrity
    • Generate flashcards and multiple choice questions. Upload lecture slides, textbooks, or videos, and Anara will create flashcards and practice quizzes tailored to your specific materials.
    • Build a single, searchable library for all your projects. Whether working solo or collaborating with a team, Anara provides one organized place for all your scholarly work.

    ​
    Getting started

    To get started with Anara, you can do any of the following:

    Import

    Add documents, images, audio and video to your library

    Chat

    Get answers to questions from the internet and your documents

    Write

    Write, edit and cite in our editor

    Teams

    Collaborate with your team
